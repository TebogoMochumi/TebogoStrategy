About Tebogo

My Name is Tebogo Mochumi a 24 year old honey from Krugersdorp, west of Johannesburg. I studied Psychology and found myself working in a sales job within a retail space. With my sales and marketing background I took on learning short online courses to upskill myself on digital marketing. I completed a marketing management course through the South African College of Business and I also completed a Google digital marketing course, which I both got certification for. I have adapted problem solving skills through the way in which I analyse situations. This has allowed me to learn Human Centered Design at Umuzi which is a design thinking system I can apply with solving problems. My current focus is on strategy and being able to incoporate certain elements into finding necessary solutions.

"To live is an act of courage."

My mission is to focus on digital marketing strategies and how to build brands online. With a strong focus on digital marketing tools and google analytics systems, brands can be analysed and positioned within the right spaces.
